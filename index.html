About Me

Professional Editor & Designer with 20+ Years Experience

I'm Ana Lee, a passionate editor and designer dedicated to helping clients transform their ideas into polished, professional content that resonates with their audience.

My journey began at a major publishing house, where I spent 5 years honing my skills in developmental editing, copy editing, and proofreading across various genres. This experience gave me a deep understanding of the publishing process and what makes content truly shine.

After discovering my love for visual storytelling, I expanded into graphic design, completing formal training at the School of Visual Arts. Now I combine these two passions to offer comprehensive services that address both the content and visual presentation of your project.
